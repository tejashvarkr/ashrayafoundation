Skill Development

Empowering women through skill-building programs.

Our program is designed to empower women by teaching them practical skills that can be used to earn a sustainable livelihood.

Our Skill Development programs are dedicated to empowering women by providing them with practical training and opportunities to achieve financial independence. Through various initiatives, we focus on enhancing their skills and boosting their confidence.

  • Tailoring courses & Beautician courses
  • Terrace gardening
  • Computer training

These programs include free tailoring courses, where participants learn stitching, fabric cutting, and pattern-making, and beautician courses that cover hairstyling, makeup, and skincare. Additionally, we conduct terrace gardening workshops in collaboration with the Department of Horticulture, teaching women how to grow organic vegetables and fruits, while self-help groups and leadership programs help women develop financial literacy and access government schemes. To ensure women stay competitive in a digital world, we also offer computer training sessions and distribute computers to those in need
